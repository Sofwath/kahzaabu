Maldivian statutory law

The canonical archive of Maldivian statutes lives at old.mvlaw.gov.mv, published by the Attorney General's Office. Kahzaabu links out to it rather than reimporting the content. See ADR 0012 for the rationale.

The Constitution is bundled with kahzaabu as a one-time text snapshot (parsed pre-ADR-0012) and is searchable inline at /constitution. For anything else — Acts, regulations, repealed laws, publications — follow the links below to the AGO's canonical source.

Privacy note: any search you type here is sent to your chosen engine's servers before reaching mvlaw.gov.mv — DuckDuckGo (default) does not log it, Google does. For 100% private browsing, click any tile below directly: each is a static link to the AGO domain with no third party involved.

Why kahzaabu links out instead of importing

mvlaw.gov.mv's robots.txt invokes Article 4 of EU Directive 2019/790 as an express reservation of rights for AI-input and AI-training uses. Kahzaabu is a fact-checking pipeline that feeds its corpus to LLMs — exactly that use case.

The link-out approach honors that reservation by:

  • Never fetching, parsing, or storing mvlaw.gov.mv content in kahzaabu's database.
  • Sending users to the canonical source where attribution and current-as-of dates are correctly presented by the AGO.
  • Keeping kahzaabu's posture toward third-party rights-holders explicit and auditable (see docs/adr/0012-mvlaw-link-out-not-scrape.md).

If you have an inline fact-check that needs to quote statutory text, follow the link, copy the relevant section, and paste it into the fact-check's evidence_quotes JSON with explicit attribution. Same model kahzaabu uses for any other external quotation.
